transportation

ARA robots transport tools and other supplies autonomously around the horse stable. They navigate tight spaces and deliver items quickly, ensuring a smooth and organized workflow.

riding area care

ARA robots, efficiently smooth riding arenas by using 3D LiDAR sensors and AI algorithms to analyze the surface and navigate the arena. ARA's precision and adaptability allow it to autonomously adjust to different arena conditions, whether it’s compacting the ground or distributing footing material for optimal riding quality. This makes maintaining the arena both easier and more effective, saving time and labor.
